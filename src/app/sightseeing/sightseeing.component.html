The Chapel Bridge in Lucerne is a landmark and one of the oldest wooden bridges in Europe, built around 1365. It spans the Reuss River and is famous for its historical roof paintings depicting Swiss history and local saints. After a devastating fire in 1993, the bridge was restored and reopened. Matterhorn, Wallis

The Matterhorn, one of the most famous mountains in the Alps, is located on the border between Switzerland and Italy. Standing at 4,478 meters, it is renowned for its distinctive pyramid shape. The mountain attracts numerous climbers and tourists each year. The Matterhorn is considered a symbol of Switzerland and represents the majestic beauty of the Alps.

Located on Lake Zurich, the city features a picturesque old town with historic buildings, numerous museums, and art galleries.
